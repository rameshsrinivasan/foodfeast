Who we are

A story of commitment and preciseness

Opened in 1988, The Restaurant is a casual fine dining restaurant, offering eclectic American ,Indian,Spanish cuisine and warm hospitality in a rustic historically preserved building. A high, open beam ceiling, exposed brick and concrete floors display the original space, while an open kitchen, banquette seating and gentle lighting contribute to an intimate and contemporary feel. Our food is sourced from top quality seasonal ingredients, featuring a high percentage of local and organic products. Our menu changes every six to eight weeks, offering a mix of new and signature dishes.
